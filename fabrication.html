How Fabrication Requests Work

Custom work usually starts with a review. From there, scope, hours, materials, and timeline can be confirmed before any approved fabrication begins.

Step 01

Send the idea

Share what you need built, fixed, modified, or prototyped.

Step 02

Review the scope

Measurements, photos, sketches, materials, and goals are reviewed.

Step 03

Approve the work

Estimated hours and material needs are confirmed before fabrication.

Step 04

Build it

Approved fabrication work is billed at $125/hr plus agreed material costs.

What to Include With a Request

The more detail you provide, the easier it is to estimate the work accurately. A simple sketch or photo is often enough to start.

Measurements Width, height, depth, hole spacing, material thickness, or rough dimensions.
Materials Metal, wood, plastic, hardware, finish expectations, or material preferences.
Use case What the part needs to do, where it will live, and what forces or conditions it may see.
